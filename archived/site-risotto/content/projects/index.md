**Role:** Lead Gameplay Engineer | **Timeline:** 2024 – Present

Led development of combat architecture (GAS, 7-stage damage pipeline, direction-aware parry), hybrid AI framework (50+ concurrent entities at 60 FPS), and 19+ custom plugins powering a modular, scalable gameplay foundation.
